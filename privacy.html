Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-12

Market Pin does not require accounts and is designed not to collect personal data.

What the app does

  • Requests market quote and chart data from Yahoo Finance endpoints over HTTPS.
  • Stores a local appearance preference on-device using UserDefaults.

What the app does not do

  • No tracking across apps or websites.
  • No analytics SDK or advertising SDK.
  • No account, sign-in, or user profile.
  • No sale or sharing of personal data.

Data retention and deletion

Market Pin does not collect or store personal data on any server. The only data stored locally is your appearance preference (automatic, light, or dark), saved by macOS via UserDefaults. You can delete this at any time by removing the app.

Third parties

The app fetches publicly available market data from Yahoo Finance over HTTPS. No personal information is sent to Yahoo Finance or any other third party. Please refer to Yahoo's own privacy policy for their data practices.

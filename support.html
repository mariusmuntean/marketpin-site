Frequently asked questions

How do I change the tracked symbol?
Symbol configuration will be available in a future settings UI. In the meantime, quit the app and relaunch with a different symbol configured via the environment variable.
Why is the price delayed?
Market data is sourced from Yahoo Finance and may be delayed by up to 15 minutes depending on the exchange. This is a limitation of the data source, not the app.
The app shows "€ --" — what's wrong?
The app could not reach Yahoo Finance. Check your internet connection and try refreshing with ⌘R.
What keyboard shortcuts are available?
⌘R refreshes quote and chart data, Esc closes the popover, and ⌘Q quits the app.
How do I change the appearance?
Click the theme icon in the bottom-left of the popover to switch between automatic, light, and dark appearance.

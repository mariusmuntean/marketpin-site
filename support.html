Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the tracked symbol?
Quit the app, relaunch with a different symbol configured, or wait for a settings UI in a future version.
Why is the price delayed?
Market data is sourced from Yahoo Finance and may be delayed by up to 15 minutes depending on the exchange.
The app shows "€ --" — what's wrong?
The app could not reach Yahoo Finance. Check your internet connection and try refreshing with ⌘R.
